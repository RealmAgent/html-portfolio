Top 3 Movies According to Kyle

My top 3 movies of all time

Catch Me If You Can

Steven Spielberg at his best. Tom Hanks at his best. Leonardo Dicaprio at his best.

The soundtrack is excilerating and moves the movie along at a riveting pace. The story constantly unfolds as Carol closes in on Frank, the con-man.

Knives Out

Daniel Craig's masterpiece performance. A fun mystery with a stellar cast and fun original story with twists and turns.

Bull Durham

If anything can capture the obsessive pull that sports can have on a man, it's this movie.

to the minor leagues, Crash Davis continues chasing the dream. The dream of improvement and validation in the form of a home run or a hard hit line drive. Maybe it's the ballpark or the locker room and traveling on the bus with the guys. It's all part of the allure of the game.
